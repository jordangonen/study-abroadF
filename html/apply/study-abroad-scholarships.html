  • Rowe Summer Study Abroad Scholarship

    For Washington University students demonstrating a high level of financial need and participating in a WUSTL-administered program for which no other funding sources are available. Preference is given to students participating in Washington University faculty-led programs and/or programs outside Western Europe. Awards up to $2,500.

  • Eisler Summer Study Abroad Scholarship

    For Washington University students demonstrating a high level of financial need and participating in a WUSTL-administered programs for which no other funding sources are available. Preference given to students participating in Washington University faculty-led programs and/or programs outside Western Europe. Awards up to $1,500

  • WU in Shanghai Semester Scholarship

    Students planning to study on the WU in Shanghai (in affiliation with Fudan University) program for either the fall or spring semester program are eligible to apply. Those studying for the summer only are not eligible for this scholarship. Awards up to $1,500

  • WU in Shanghai Summer + Fall Grants

    Students planning to study consecutive semesters on the WU Summer Chinese Language Institute program and WU in Shanghai (in affiliation with Fudan University) program fall semester are eligible to apply. Awards up to $1,500
